Math 231: Calculus II

Overview

Second course in calculus and analytic geometry: techniques of integration, conic sections, polar coordinates, and infinite series.

Credit Hours: 3 Hours

Requirements:

Prerequisite: Calculus I (or equivalent)

Courseware: This course uses the Mathable online system, which costs $99. Please see our Mathable Overview page for more information.

Tuition and Registration Fee

Undergraduate: $1227

Graduate: $1377


Please note that the above rates include a $50 per credit hour non-refundable fee for course registration.

Course Options

This page has information regarding the self-paced, rolling enrollment course. If you are a UIUC student interested in taking a course during the summer, you may be interested in a Summer Session II course. Please see our Summer Course Options Guide for more information.

Mentor

Individual students enrolled in this course are assigned to a course mentor.

Testing

Exams: This course has two 90-minute mid term tests, a 60-minute Mastery test and a 3-hour final exam.

Proctorship Information: This course requires that you obtain a proctor. Please see our Proctor Information page for further instructions.

Course Timeline

Non-High School Students: Your time in the course begins on the date your registration is processed. This course is 16 weeks long with the possibility of purchasing an extension.


Eligible students may purchase a single 2-month extension for $300.


Students with a Bachelor's degree will be assessed graduate level tuition rate for this course. However, one cannot receive graduate level credit for courses numbered below 400 at the University of Illinois.
